SF2000

What is the SF2000?

The SF2000 is a cheap hand-held emulation gaming console sold by a variety of vendors.

Features

Display

The display is an 2.8" IPS (not OCA laminated) panel with a 240x320 resolution rotated 90Â° clockwise to give a 320x240 display.

Controls

Buttons

ABXY SNES controller-like membrane buttons.

D-Pad

SNES controller-like membrane D-Pad and a SELECT and a START button.

Thumb Stick

The thumb stick only usable for 8 directions (up, down, lft, right and 4 diagonals) and is not depressable.
